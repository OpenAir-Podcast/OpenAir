What's new in OpenAir 0.16.4:

• Sleep timer - Set a timer to automatically stop playback
• Share button - Easily share episodes from the player controls
• Queue navigation - Quick access to your playback queue from the player
• Author information - Now displayed in downloads, favorites, and history
• Favorite button - Add episodes to favorites directly from the main player
• Improved feed management - Clear feed option when you have no subscriptions
• Various UI improvements and bug fixes
